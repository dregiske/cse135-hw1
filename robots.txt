**Basic robots.txt:**
```
User-agent: *
Allow: /

Sitemap: https://andregiske.com/sitemap.xml
```

**Details:**
- `User-agent: *` - Applies to all search engine bots
- `Allow: /` - Allow crawling of all pages
- `Sitemap:` - Optional, tells bots where your sitemap is
